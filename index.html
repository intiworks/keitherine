Commissions

Keitherine's commission info

Thank you for taking interest in my art! Here’s all you need to know about commissioning me, so please take your time to read. For any other questions you might have, you can contact me through my contact form or email.

Guidelines

I usually draw
  • Original characters
  • Real people in my style
  • Fanart
  • Twitch emotes/avatars
  • ...and much more!



How does it work?

After the payment is confirmed I will start working on a rough sketch, either digital or traditional. This part of the process will require your help to make sure everything is in line with what you have envisioned.
After we have nailed down the general themes and ideas in the sketch, I will begin working on the lineart. I will periodically ask for your feedback to ensure everything is to your liking and is consistent with previously discussed themes. The more detailed you are with what you want, the easier it will be to match your expectations, reference pictures are especially helpful with this.
Once the commission is finished, and everything is to your liking, I will send you the finished piece via email to preserve image quality.

Customization options

  • You can choose face angle, expression, pose, etc.
  • If it’s something too specific, I will need a reference picture to work with, especially when doing complex poses.
  • If you need a specific canvas/image resolution size, let me know and I will work on that resolution for you. Format can be .jpg or .png. That is also up to you.
The more detailed you are with what you want, the better.
Reference pictures are always welcome. Give me as much guidance and details about what you want so we can come up with what you have in mind. This will save us a lot of time too. I want you to be happy with the final artwork!

Terms of service

  • I only accept PayPal, and the payment must be done upfront, in full. I won’t work on the artwork until the payment is confirmed. If the price of your commission(s) exceeds $100, you can do half upfront if you prefer.
  • No refunds or cancellations.
  • Commissions are intended for personal use only. Do not edit my work and/or claim it as your own. If you need it for commercial use, we can talk about a price for you to purchase full copyright.
  • Be patient. I always try my best to not keep you waiting, but I don’t like rushing my work. Art takes time.
